Our Amazon Wishlist

Prefer to send physical supplies instead of a monetary donation? We go through massive amounts of food, cleaning supplies, and toys every week. Sending an item from our wishlist helps us keep the ranch running smoothly!

Our Most Frequently Needed Items

These are the items we are constantly running out of. Every donation helps directly improve the lives of our rescues.

🧹 Cleaning Supplies

Bleach, paper towels, heavy-duty trash bags, and dog-safe floor cleaners. Keeping the ranch sterile and clean is our biggest daily task!

🥩 High-Quality Dog Food

We specifically need Purina Pro Plan or Science Diet for sensitive stomachs. Our medically needy dogs require top-tier nutrition to heal.

🎾 Tough Chew Toys

Kong toys, durable ropes, and heavy-duty chew bones. Toys are essential for mental stimulation and preventing anxiety in the kennels.

🛏️ Blankets & Puppy Pads

Fleece blankets, XL pee pads, and soft towels. These keep our puppies and senior dogs warm, comfortable, and dry.
