# July 2026: Freedom and Furry Friends

Dear Safe Haven Supporters,

Happy July! As we celebrate independence and freedom this month, we reflect on what those words mean for the dogs here at the ranch. For our rescues, freedom means breaking the chains of neglect, leaving the confines of a concrete kennel, and finally having the freedom to just be a dog.

**Freedom for Buster**
This month, we took in Buster, a profoundly deaf young Dalmatian mix who was lost and in danger. Thanks to your support, we were able to get him a vibration collar and enroll him in specialized training to learn hand signals. He is thriving! Your donations give dogs like Buster the freedom to learn and communicate in a world that wasn't built for them.

**Summer Heat Safety Warning**
With the July heat bearing down, please remember that if the pavement is too hot for your bare hands, it’s too hot for their paws! We are currently looking for donations of kiddie pools, frozen treats, and heavy-duty shade tarps to keep our residents cool during the dog days of summer.

**Dog of the Month: Rosie**

Warmly,

# October 2026: Spooky Season and Sweet Saves

Dear Safe Haven Community,

October brings cooler weather, pumpkin-spice everything, and a flurry of activity here at the ranch. While the season might be "spooky," there's nothing scary about the incredible transformations we've witnessed this month.

**Black Dog Syndrome is Real**
Did you know that black dogs are historically the hardest to adopt and the first to be euthanized in overcrowded shelters? It's a sad phenomenon known as "Black Dog Syndrome." This month, we're shining a spotlight on our beautiful black residents, like Shadow, a sweet 6-year-old Lab mix who was surrendered when his family had a baby. Let's break the stigma and show these dogs that their lives matter just as much! 

**Howl-o-Ween Costume Contest!**
Get your costumes ready! Our annual Howl-o-Ween Costume Contest and Parade is happening on Saturday, October 24th. We'll have categories for Best Owner/Dog Duo, Spookiest Pup, and Most Creative. Entry is a $10 donation, and all proceeds go toward our winter heating fund for the kennels. 

**Dog of the Month: Pumpkin**
Appropriately named for the season, Pumpkin is a 7-year-old Pomeranian who survived a puppy mill. She is a fluffy orange survivor who is learning how to be a real dog for the very first time. She needs a quiet, patient home to help her build confidence. Could you be her safe haven?

**Winter Wishlist**

Thank you for being the magic that makes these rescues possible. We wish you a happy, safe, and treat-filled Halloween!

**Thanksgiving Safety Reminders**
As you celebrate Thanksgiving, please remember that many human foods are dangerous for dogs. Keep turkey bones, onions, garlic, grapes, and chocolate far out of reach. If you want to treat your pup, stick to a spoonful of plain, canned pumpkin or a piece of plain cooked turkey!
