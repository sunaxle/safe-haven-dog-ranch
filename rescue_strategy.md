# Rescue Strategy & Priorities - Summer 2026 through Winter 2027

## High-Level Summary of Recent Updates
The team has now completed outreach planning and content generation through January 2027. The central theme for the winter months is the "Home for the Holidays" fostering program and preparing the ranch for severe freezing temperatures. 2026 was a record-breaking year with 540 adoptions, and the goal is to carry that momentum into 2027 by expanding the foster network. Web development continues on Volunteer Forms after the successful launch of the Adoption and Donation portals.
