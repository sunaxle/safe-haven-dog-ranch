Special Needs Outreach Program

Empowering young adults in our community through connection, real-world experiences, and the healing power of dogs.

A Partnership with Purpose

At Safe Haven Dog Ranch, we believe that everyone deserves a chance to shine. Our secondary mission is dedicated to working with special needs young adults in our community.

Many of these incredible individuals, recent high school graduates or students in their final year, face challenges in gaining the volunteer and work experiences that make them desirable to employers. We are here to change that.

The Mercedes ISD Pilot Program

We have partnered with special education teachers to bring students out to the ranch for hands-on, real-world learning. They engage in activities such as:

  • Walking and socializing with the rescue dogs
  • Basic ranch maintenance and plumbing repairs
  • Creating handmade dog treats and bracelets for our shop
  • Graphic design and creative projects (like our logo!)
