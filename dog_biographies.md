# New Arrivals - June 2026

## Barnaby
**Breed:** Golden Retriever Mix  
**Age:** ~8 years old  
**Background:** Barnaby was found tied to a park bench in the pouring rain, confused and waiting for a family that never returned. When he first arrived at Safe Haven, he wouldn't lift his head, his tail tucked permanently between his legs. 

But old goldens have a resilience forged in pure love. Over the past three weeks, Barnaby has slowly unfurled. He loves nothing more than a slow morning stroll and resting his heavy, graying muzzle on your knee for a chin scratch. He's looking for a quiet retirement home where he can finally understand that he is safe, he is loved, and he will never be left behind again.

## Luna
**Breed:** Pitbull / Terrier Mix  
**Age:** 2 years old  
**Background:** They call her the "Tripod Tornado." Luna lost her front left leg to an old, untreated injury before she was rescued from a high-kill shelter just hours before her time was up. You would think losing a limb would slow her down. You would be wrong.

Luna is a beacon of pure, undiluted joy. She does zoomies with the grace of a gazelle and has a smile that stretches from ear to flopping ear. She doesn't know she's different; she just knows that every human she meets is a potential best friend. Luna needs an active family who will throw tennis balls until their arms fall off, and who will give her the softest bed to crash on afterwards.

## Pip
**Breed:** Chihuahua Mix  
**Age:** 6 months old  
**Background:** Pip weighs exactly four pounds, and three of those pounds are pure ears. He was discovered by a good Samaritan in a taped-up cardboard box outside a grocery store. Trembling and terrified, Pip spent his first few days with us hiding under his blankets.

Now, this tiny warrior is finding his voice. He has decided he is the fiercest protector of his foster mom's slippers and takes his guard duties very seriously. Pip is a fragile little soul who needs a gentle, patient adopter to show him that the world is much kinder than the cardboard box he came from.

## Buster
**Breed:** Dalmatian Mix  
**Age:** 1.5 years old  
**Background:** Buster was found wandering a rural highway, a striking boy completely oblivious to the danger around him. We soon realized why he didn't react to the honking cars: Buster is completely deaf. 

Living in a silent world hasn't dampened his spirit one bit. He is highly intelligent, eager to please, and already learning hand signals! He watches you with intense focus, trying to understand what you want. Buster needs a patient, dedicated family—ideally with another confident dog to act as his "hearing guide"—who will continue his training and show him that love is a language that doesn't need to be heard.

## Daisy
**Breed:** Yorkshire Terrier  
**Age:** 12 years old  
**Background:** Daisy came to us from an overwhelmed hoarding situation. She was matted, frightened, and had terrible dental disease. After a spa day and some necessary vet care (she’s now rockin' a toothless smile!), a completely different dog emerged.

This tiny senior has an incredibly spunk. She practically dances for her soft food and insists on being carried like the royalty she is. Daisy has had a hard life, and she deserves a retirement filled with plush beds, gentle handling, and an endless supply of love. If you have a quiet home and a lap that needs warming, Daisy is your girl.
