Where Your Donation Goes

🏥

Medical Care

Vaccinations, spay/neuter surgeries, heartworm treatments, and emergency vet visits for severely neglected dogs.

🥩

Nutrition & Board

High-quality food, treats, supplements, and maintaining a clean, safe ranch environment for our dogs.

🎾

Rehabilitation

Training, behavioral therapy, toys, and socialization programs to prepare them for their forever families.
